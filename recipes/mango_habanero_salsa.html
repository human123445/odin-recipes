Mango Habanero Salsa

I'm still fiddling with the recipe. Feel free to play around with it. I've been testing different tomatoes!

Tools You Need.

  • Oven
  • Baking Sheet
  • Food Processor (Blender will aslo work)

Ingredients

  • 4 Tomatoes (vine on)
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 3 Habanero Peppers
  • 2 Jalapenos (bigger the bettter)
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Can of Corn (frozen corn will also work)
  • Cilantro (A handful)
  • 1 Lime (juice)
  • Salt (to taste)

Prep

  1. Set your oven to broil.
  2. Slice the tomatoes and onion in half and set them aside on a baking sheet.
  3. Destem the habanero peppers and jalapenos, slice the jalapenos in half long ways and place them on the baking sheet.
  4. Destem and deseed the red pepper, cut it long ways into forths and place the slices on the baking sheet.
  5. Cut the mango in half, removing the pit (throw the pit away). Place the mango on the baking sheet (skin facing down towards the baking sheet).
  6. Chop your cilantro and place it in the food processor. Cut your lime in half and sqeeze the juice in the processor too!

Broil

  1. Place the baking sheet in the oven for 15 minutes. (I like to use the shelf 2nd from the top for that crispy taste).
  2. Use the rest of the time to cook your corn in a saucepan, then add it to the food processor. (Use either butter or vegetable oil for a Vegan Option)
  3. After 15 minutes remove the habaneros from the oven and place them in the food processor. Put the pan back in the oven for 5 minutes.

Blend

  1. Take the pan out of the oven.
  2. Peel the skin off of the Mangos and tomatoes. CAREFUL VERY HOT!
  3. Add everything into the food processor (add salt to taste)
  4. Blend until you get a nice consistancy (I like to pulse for about 15 seconds).

Enjoy!
